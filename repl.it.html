Purpose of the Game


The purpose of the game is for the player to guess a word by entering letters into the program. With each incorrect guess, the man on the noose 
gains another limb until he has all 6 body parts and the player loses the game. One key feature of the game is the capability of selecting the difficult before guessing 
a word. Other key features include the drawings of the gallow and the man and as well as being able to choose whether or not to play again.
